--- source: The Private Diary of Dr. John Dee ---

I delivered my letters to Mr. Thomas Kelley for his brother Sir Edward Kelley, knight, at the Emperor’s court at Prage. Francys Garland was by, and Mr. Thomas Kelley his wife. God send them well thither and hither agayn! Mr. Emery had disbursed to me frankly betwene the tyme from Shrovetyde tyll this May £25.
