--- source: The Private Diary of Dr. John Dee --- to Isabell Boordman 8s. 8d. to make up whole yere’s wagis due at the Annunciation of our Lady last past. I allowed to Mr. Williamson ten dayes respite more for his kinsman to bring in his evidence for the process of the proceedings. Payd to nurse 3s. to make up her full payment of her yere’s wagis ended at Michaelmas last.
