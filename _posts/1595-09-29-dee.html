--- source: The Private Diary of Dr. John Dee --- Margery Stubble of Hownslow, our dry nurse, entred into 54 the yere of her servyce begynning on Michaelmas Day, and is to have £3 her yeres wagis and a gown cloth of russet. Edward Edwards began his yere of serving me allso on Michelmas Day, and he must have 40s. for his yere’s wagis, and a lyvery.
