{{ "Dee, John" | person_tag: "My" }} dream of being naked, and my skyn all overwrowght with work like some kinde of tuft mockado, with crosses blew and red; and on my left arme, abowt the arme, in a wreath, this word I red — sine me nihil potestis facere: and another the same night of {{ "Walsingham, Francis" | person_tag: "Mr. Secretary Walsingham" }}, {{ "Cavendish, Thomas" | person_tag: "Mr. Candish" }}, and myself.
