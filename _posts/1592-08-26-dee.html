--- source: The Private Diary of Dr. John Dee --- Mr. Heriot 40s. Auditor Hill, £4. Remember all thing is payd to our nurse at Barnes for the girle Francys Dee from hir birth untyll the ende of her eight month, lacking 12s., and on Sunday, the 27th of this August, we so concluded, when we gave the nurse ten shillings. The eight month ended (from Newyere’s day morning last) the 12th of this month.
