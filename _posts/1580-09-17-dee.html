{{ "Queen Elizabeth" | person_tag: "The Quene’s Majestie" }} cam from {{ "Richmond Palace, England" | place_tag: "Rychemond" }} in her coach, the higher way of Mortlak felde, and whan she cam right against {{ "St Mary the Virgin Mortlake, England" | place_tag: "the church" }} she turned down toward {{ "Dee's house, Mortlake, England" | place_tag: "my howse" }}: and when she was against my garden in the felde she stode there a good while, and than cam ynto the street at the great gate of the felde, where she espyed me at my doore making obeysains to her Majestie; she beckend her hand for me; I cam to her coach side, she very speedily pulled off her glove and gave me her hand to kiss; and to be short, asked me to resort to her court, and to give her to wete when I cam ther; hor. 6¼ a meridie.
