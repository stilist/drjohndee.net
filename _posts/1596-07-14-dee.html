--- source: The Private Diary of Dr. John Dee --- July 10th to 14th, occupyed with low controversies, as with Holden of Salford and the tenants of Sir John Byron of Faylsworth in the right of the colledge, sending to ..... to the cownty, and sending for Mr. Tyldesley or Chester for cownsaylers. Mr. Saxton rode away. The sessions day at Manchester.
